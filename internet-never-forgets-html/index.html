Stay Safe Online

Protect your digital footprint with these essential tips

Manage Your Digital Footprint

Regularly audit your online presence. Google yourself and see what information is publicly available.

  • Review privacy settings on all platforms
  • Remove or update outdated profiles
  • Monitor your online reputation

Think Before You Post

Consider the long-term implications of everything you share online. Would you be comfortable with this content being public forever?

  • Pause before posting emotional content
  • Avoid sharing personal information
  • Consider your future self

Use Privacy Tools

Leverage available tools and settings to protect your privacy and control your digital presence.

  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Use VPNs for browsing
  • Regularly clear cookies and cache

Stay Informed

Keep up with privacy laws, platform policies, and digital rights to make informed decisions.

  • Understand GDPR and data rights
  • Read platform terms of service
  • Follow digital privacy news
